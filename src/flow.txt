Project Overview
The proposed platform is a digital job marketplace designed to connect professionals and
employers.
The platform will allow:
● Candidates to create profiles, search jobs, and apply
● Employers to register, post job listings, and search candidate profiles
● Admins to verify job postings before they go live
● The website can be easily translated in local language with a click
The goal is to create a centralized hiring platform for professionals, simplifying recruitment and
job discovery.
Project Objectives
The platform aims to:
● Provide a simple job discovery experience for professionals
● Enable employers to easily post and manage job listings
● Allow employers to search candidate profiles
● Ensure quality job listings through admin verification
● Provide dashboards for employers and candidates to manage their
activities Additional monetization features:
● Employers can promote job postings as paid advertisements depending on the
number of days selected.
